Wuz Uuuup! Welcome to the Stellar Blade GX browser mod for Opera GX! While this mod
is still in its early stages, it will soon include a variety of features inspired
by the Stellar Blade video game—such as light and dark wallpapers, custom keyboard
and browser sounds, themed icons, stickers, and music straight from the game.

I’ve also added a simple CSS tweak that modifies text and background colors
specifically on pages within the youtube.com domain. I'm currently exploring
experimental features like custom cursors and other advanced options that may not
be widely accessible (and might not even work for me yet). Finally, I’m considering
adding basic shaders—once I find a practical use case for them!
